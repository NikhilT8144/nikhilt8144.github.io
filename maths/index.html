Algebra

Algebra deals with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. It includes everything from solving elementary equations to studying abstractions such as groups, rings, and fields. Know More!

Geometry

Geometry involves the study of shapes, sizes, and the properties of space. Topics include points, lines, planes, angles, circles, and various geometric figures. Know More!

Calculus

Calculus focuses on change and motion; it involves derivatives and integrals. It is used to understand how things change and to calculate areas under curves. Know More!

Trigonometry

Trigonometry studies relationships involving lengths and angles of triangles. It includes the study of sine, cosine, tangent, and their applications. Know More!

Statistics

Statistics is the study of data: how to collect, analyze, interpret, and present it. It is used to make decisions based on data and to predict future trends. Know More!
